About

ProEntertainment was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate film enthusiasts who envisioned a new standard for movie theaters. Starting with a single location in the heart of the city, we quickly became a favorite among locals for our superior sound and visual quality. Today, ProEntertainment boasts over 20 theaters nationwide, each designed to provide an unparalleled cinematic experience. Our journey has been marked by a dedication to innovation, quality, and community, and we look forward to continuing our legacy for years to come.
